Honest Inquiry

Inquiry Requires Discipline.

Four questions keep certainty from outrunning evidence.

Whenever you encounter a claim, pause. Ask: What do we know? What do we reasonably infer? What remains possible? What remains unknown? Curiosity without discipline becomes fantasy; discipline without curiosity becomes dogma. Apotheosis protects wonder while respecting reason, evidence, and the courage to say, “I do not yet know.”
