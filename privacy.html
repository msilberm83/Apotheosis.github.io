Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026

Apotheosis Church respects your privacy. This policy explains the information we collect, why we use it, when it may be disclosed, and the measures used to protect it.

Information we collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, account or membership details, messages, donation information, purchase information, and participation preferences. Our website and service providers may also collect technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, referring pages, and basic usage or security logs.

Payments

Membership payments, donations, and purchases may be processed by Stripe or another disclosed payment provider. Payment-card details are submitted directly to the payment provider and are not stored by Apotheosis Church. The provider processes information under its own privacy policy and security practices.

How we use information

We use information to provide memberships and requested services; process payments, donations, and purchases; communicate about the Church and its activities; respond to questions; maintain records; prevent fraud and misuse; improve the website; and comply with legal obligations.

How information is disclosed

We do not sell personal information. We may disclose information to service providers that help operate the website, communications, payments, accounting, or security; when you direct or consent to disclosure; to protect rights, safety, and property; or when required by law. Service providers receive only the information reasonably necessary for their work.

Cookies and third-party services

The website and linked services may use cookies or similar technologies for essential operation, security, preferences, and payment sessions. Following a link to a third-party service makes that service’s terms and privacy policy applicable.

Retention and security

We retain information only as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above and for legal, accounting, or dispute-resolution needs. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information we maintain, subject to applicable law and necessary recordkeeping. You may opt out of nonessential communications at any time. Subscription billing can be managed through the customer portal linked from the Members page.

Children

Our online services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information online without appropriate consent.

Changes and contact

We may update this policy and will post the revised effective date here. Questions or privacy requests may be sent to mike@apotheosischurch.org, through our contact page, or by mail to Apotheosis Church, 672 S. Camino Real, #1, Palm Springs, CA 92264.
